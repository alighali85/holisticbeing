Holisting Living


Lama has always been enjoying interdisciplinary studies in multicultural environments at a global level. However, the nature, well being, and health of human beings have been important jewels in his approaches to life. Focusing on the holistic human nature of body, mind, soul, spirit and connections, he studied food science, nutrition, metaphysics, meditation and holistic health. Other interests and experiences include holistic therapy, NLP and hypnosis, mysticism, ancient civilizations, Zen, Buddhism, and Sufism. Friends describe Lama as a compassionate, joyful, and humorous soul fulfilled with positive and healing energies, which are experienced by everybody around him! According to his birthday, the Mayan astrology describes him: “A galactic tone of 12 and sacred sign of storm/rain (19) allow Lama to enjoy wide range of qualities such, vitality, keen intelligence with which to analyze and order his experience; healing knowledge; learning and teaching, wholeness and the capability of putting the pieces together when gaining new understanding or experience; and Lama will never lose his childlike innocence and curiosity”.
