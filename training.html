Our approach

Holistic living is a way of exploring the profound within and around us

Modern life is ego centered which tends to emphasize thinking and neglect body movement and emotions. As a result, modern man is lost, stressed and tired running behind illusions such as power, prestige, material possessions, competitions, and the approval of others.

Effective approach

An effective approach to solve these problems is one, which allows the inner wisdom of every individual to blossom, takes current health research findings into consideration, and harnesses the accumulated experience of traditional methods while incorporating fresh participatory methods. This naturally involves a synthesis of several approaches according to the needs of the target individual and groups, and the specific goals and learning environment at hand.



deep trust

In such an environment of deep trust between the seeker and the healer, communication beyond physical expressions is born on the level of being. As a result, you learn how to silence your mind peacefully, release blocked energies and traumas un-judgmentally, and express your emotions freely. These unconfined energies will allow you to liberate yourself, become more meditative, live healthier, and experience more joy and fun in every area of your life.

The focal point of our services is to support people empower themselves with the skills and confidence necessary to manage various aspects of their lives physically, emotionally, spiritually, and socially, particularly in case of illnesses.

inner wisdom

In touch with his own inner wisdom and working with people in a space of awareness, meditation, and love, as a friend, Lama helps in creating the proper education and environment in which the inner wisdom of healing and growth is allowed to flourish and where current and previous wounds are recognized, healed and left in peace.

Knowledge

Knowledge can be transferred but at best it only contains a marginal part of the authentic learning process. On the other side understanding does not depend on the transfer of information from one person to the other but it is much more subtle and deep where it connects one with the universal SOURCE of all which exists in every being. In this context individuals can truly recognize their true unique nature where nothing is left to be taught!

hand-on experience

Through hand-on experience Lama guides and facilitates the personal and holistic advancement of the individuals and groups who sincerely seek understanding, inner contentment and meaning in their lives. Sometimes he encourages the individual reflections on thoughts and emotions, and most of the time he supports the no-thinking process as an important and integral part of a worthwhile growth. He assists you in recognizing the best ways to apply the acquired understanding into real life issues such as finding new meanings and experiencing life-long joy in personal life, relationships, school and work.


How do we help!

Lama provides public lectures, seminars, individual holistic coaching

which inspire you to further understand the different levels of your being, your life purpose and how to live joyfully moment to moment in the present NOW and in the every NOW to come. He offers educational and healing trainings designed to define and express the deep desires in one’s heart. You will learn how to know and realize your passion and purpose in life. Our healing courses can be useful for individuals and groups both for healthy people and those who suffer from chronic illnesses.


treatment sessions

Your treatment sessions are individualized for your needs. Sometimes you could be supported with hands-on healing (Reiki), supplemented by education on other natural therapies, including sound, color, aromatherapy, and other modalities, depending on your specific healing needs during the time we spend together. You will learn that it’s up to you to allow abundance into your life. It’s not about chance; it’s about making significant choices.

bring home inspirations

You will be able to bring home not only inspirations but also practical self help processes for stress management, holistic healing, and inner transformation. Lama helps you bring more inner wisdom, responsibility and individual power which will help you become more independent and liberate you from all external factors and agents, himself included.

If you desire and through your self-knowing, you can help others experience similar levels of love, healing and awareness!
